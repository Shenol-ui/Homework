So, who am I?

Early Life and Education:

I was born on January 2nd, 2005, in Skopje, and grew up in Debar, more specifically, I grew up in the small village of Dzhepishte, just south of Debar. I attended "High School 28th of November" where I developed a keen interest in technology and computers. This passion led me to pursue higher education in computer science.

Current Studies:

Currently, I am a student at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in Software Engineering. I chose this field because I love solving complex problems and creating innovative software solutions.

Interests and Hobbies:

Outside of academics, I have a wide range of interests. I enjoy football, chess, and gaming. I spend a lot of my free time doing something related to these hobbies, I find these activities help me unwind and stay creative.

Fun Facts

  • I can speak Macedonian, English and German fluently, and I can also hold a conversation in Danish.
  • I learned German through watching cartoons as a kid, because I didn't enjoy Macedonian cartoons.
  • I have been to 10 countries and love learning about new cultures

Goals and Aspirations:

In the short term, I aim to complete my degree with high honors and gain practical experience through internships. In the long term, I aspire to work with leading tech companies and eventually start my own software development firm. My ultimate goal is to create innovative solutions that can make a positive impact on society.
